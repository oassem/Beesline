 FAQ SECTION 

Click on the topics below to view our most frequently asked questions.

Step1: Log in to your account by clicking "Log in" in the header.
Step2: To add items to your basket, click on the product you wish to purchase then click "Add to Cart".
Step3: To change the desired quantity of the item, adjust the number in the "Quantity" box.
Step4: To review the items in your basket, click on the "Cart" link located at the top right of the page.
Step5: If you have an applicable promo code, enter it and click "Apply". The total price will be updated to reflect the applied promo code discount.
Step6: Review your order at the Shopping Cart Page. Once you are ready to submit your order, click "Checkout".
Step7: Fill in all the required information for your shipping and payment details and click "Place Order"

Currently, we accept payment via Cash on Delivery strictly within Egypt.
Your order confirmation number is created after successfully placing your order on shopbeesline.com.
You can find your unique number in the Order Confirmation Email received shortly after placement and also in your online account under "Login".

We aim to deliver within 2-3 business days of your order placement.
However, please note that some locations may require a longer delivery time, dependent on our delivery provider.
